About GitPub

Geeks in the Pub is a meeting of developers in Sheffield. We meet on the 1st Wednesday of every month at a different pub each month. You can find out about the next meeting at our twitter, also displayed to the right.

Geeks in the pub is organised by @davidjmemmett, @ClearCarbon and Cat.

We are currently conducting virtual events on our Discord. Watch our for annoucements via our twitter and join our discord!.
