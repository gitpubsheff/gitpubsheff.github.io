About

Geeks in the Pub is a meeting of every and any type of geek in Sheffield. We meet on the 1st Wednesday of every month at a different pub each month. You can find out about the next meeting either at this site, twitter (@ClearCarbon, @richardathome, @davidjmemmett) or google+.
